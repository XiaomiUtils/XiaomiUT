## Privacy Policy (Datenschutzerklärung)

*In accordance with the GDPR (DSGVO)*

### 1. General Overview

This website is a non-commercial hobby project. We follow the principle of **Data Minimization**.  
We do not use cookies, we do not have user accounts, and we do not track your behavior.

### 2. Infrastructure & Data Processing

To provide this service, technical data must be processed. Here is how your data flows:

**Web Hosting (Google Firebase)**  
This site is hosted on Firebase. When you access this page, Google technically processes your IP address to deliver the files. This is necessary for the operation of the site *(Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR)*.

**API Proxy (Cloudflare Workers)**  
To bypass CORS restrictions and fetch firmware info, your search requests are routed through Cloudflare. Your IP address and request headers are processed in real-time (volatile memory) to communicate with Xiaomi's servers. We do not store or log this data.

**Xiaomi Mirrors**  
When you click a download link, you connect directly to `bigota.d.miui.com` or other official Xiaomi servers. Xiaomi's own privacy policy applies to those downloads.

### 3. Contacting Us

If you reach out via Email or Session:

- We process your ID/Email and message content only to answer your request *(Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR)*.
- Data is deleted once the inquiry is resolved, unless legal retention is required.

### 4. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

- Request information about your data.
- Request deletion or correction.

> **Note:** Since we do not store IP addresses or link them to identities, we usually have no data to provide or delete upon request.
